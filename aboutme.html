Hello! My name is Shannon Moquin!

I am a 23 year old female college student in Salt Lake Community College. I am currently going for my Associate's Degree in Visual Art and Design: Animation Emphasis. I am a visual artist who loves to express stories from animation and cartoon drawings. I am always looking for different styles of art that is appealing to how I feel currently. This also gives me the opportunity to discover my own personal art drawing style. I already feel accomplished with how unique I can make my cartoons, but I would love to improve more on them, make them more "alive".

I used to write stories, but I have a comprehension disorder that makes it harder for me to take in information as well as express it. So that means I can't really multi-task well. Plus I feel like writing is not my style of story expression, especially since I can't explain in detail of what scenes are going on. I also feel as though my writing style is souly based off of how I speak, and because I can't speak fluently, my writing doesn't look clean. At least my essays are better looking, which is a plus for the professional side of my life.
